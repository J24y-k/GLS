What is GLS Refrigeration and Air Conditioning

GLS is a trusted company that started in 2011, specializing in the installation, repair, and maintenance of cooling systems for homes and businesses across Johannesburg. We pride ourselves on professionalism, precision, and customer satisfaction.

Our mission is to ensure your systems operate efficiently and provide optimal comfort for you and your environment. We're dedicated to helping you maintain a comfortable space all year round, no matter the temperature outside.
