About Us

GLS is a proudly local refrigeration and air conditioning company with a clear mission: to keep homes, businesses, and industries running smoothly with perfectly balanced climates throughout Johannesburg.

Our Approach

At GLS, we believe in a personalized approach to every project. We start by understanding your unique cooling and heating needs, ensuring that our solutions are tailored to your specific requirements. Our focus is on designing energy efficient and durable systems that not only meet but exceed your expectations.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our team of licensed technicians bring experience and expertise to every job, ensuring that your systems are installed and maintained to the highest industry standards.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and affordable refrigeration and air conditioning solutions that enhance comfort and productivity for our clients across Johannesburg and surrounding areas.

Vision

We envision a future where climate control is smarter, more reliable, and accessible for everyone, ensuring optimal environments that promote wellbeing and efficiency in every space we serve.

Our Work

Since our inception in 2011, GLS has been at the forefront of refrigeration and air conditioning services. Our portfolio showcases our commitment to excellence and our ability to adapt to the evolving needs of our clients.
